About the Centre

The Ceylon Wudang Wushu Research Centre is dedicated to preserving, researching and promoting Wudang Martial Arts, Traditional Sri Lankan Ayurveda, Meditation, Traditional Healing, Education and Holistic Studies for future generations.

Our Research

🥋

Wudang Martial Arts

Research on traditional internal martial arts, history, philosophy, training methods and cultural heritage.

🌿

Ayurveda

Research on Traditional Sri Lankan Ayurveda, herbal medicine, natural healing and wellness practices.

🧘

Meditation

Research on meditation, Qi cultivation, breathing techniques and mindfulness for holistic wellbeing.

Training Programmes

Our training programmes are designed to develop discipline, physical ability, internal energy cultivation and traditional knowledge through a structured learning pathway.

  • Beginner Programme
  • Intermediate Programme
  • Advanced Programme
  • Instructor Programme

Ayurveda & Wellness

Integrating the wisdom of Traditional Sri Lankan Ayurveda with Wudang health preservation methods to promote physical, mental and spiritual wellbeing.

Our research focuses on preventive healthcare, herbal medicine, traditional healing methods, rehabilitation and holistic wellness through the integration of ancient knowledge and modern research.
